Rajathan Famous Recipe :Dal Batti

Ingredients:
  1. Whole wheat flour (atta) 4-5 cups

  2. Salt to taste

  3. Pure ghee- 1 Tbsp per cup of flour and additional for soaking and serving (Clarified Butter)

  4. Carom seeds (ajwain) 1/2 teaspoon

How To Make Dal Batti :

1. Mix flour, carom seeds, ghee and salt. Add sufficient water to knead it into a stiff dough. Divide the dough into portions and shape them into small balls. Place the baatis on the tray. Preheat oven to 250 C or ~480 F


2.Bake them at 250Â°C for about ten minutes. And after ten minutes, turn sides and bake for another ten minutes. This worked for me. You can always lower the temperature or change sides etc.


3.Generally they are soaked in a bowl of melted desi ghee for some time before being served but I just shallow fried them in ghee in a pan and served with ghee so that people could add as desired. Remove from bowl before serving and serve with dal and ghee.
