Kerala Famous Recipe :Mango Pudding

Ingredients:
  1. Mango Pulp – 1 cup (I used Sweetened Canned Mango Pulp)

  2. Whole Milk – 2 cups

  3. Gelatin – 1 packet (7g/1 tbsp approx)

  4. Sugar – 4 tbsp (Alter according to desired sweetness)

  5. Cardamom powder – 1/2 tsp (optional)

How To Make Mango Pudding :

1.Take 2 tbsp water in a small bowl and sprinkle gelatin. Allow it to stand for 2 minutes so that it blooms and becomes thick.

2.Combine milk, cardamom and sugar in a saucepan over medium heat.

3. Add gelatin and whisk until it dissolves completely. Do not boil the milk.

4. Remove from heat and allow the milk to cool.

5. Blend the mango pulp and cooled milk thoroughly until combined.

6. Pour into dessert bowls and refrigerate for 2 hours or until set.

7. Before serving, top each bowl with a teaspoon of mango pulp or fresh mango slices.


Note

1.You can also puree ripe mangoes in a food processor or mixie until smooth to get mango puree. Strain the puree to remove any fiber and solids.

2.Instead of Whole Milk, you can also use Evaporated Milk, Condensed Milk, Whipping Cream and so on for a creamier pudding.

3.Make sure the pudding is sweet enough before you leave it for setting.
